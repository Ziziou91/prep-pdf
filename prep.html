Preparation Steps

This PDF contains everything you’ll need to prepare for our Entry Challenge. There is no level of previous knowledge needed to complete these, we’re looking for people who can keep up with the fast-paced learning environment on our bootcamps.

The material serves 2 purposes. As well as allowing us to see if a bootcamp is right for you, it let's you have a go at learning to code and see if you enjoy it!

During your Entry Challenge, you will answer a series of questions and then work through the code with a member of our teaching team. There's no pass/fail mark, instead we're looking to see how you break down adn approach solving a problem.

Work through these resources in order and make sure you fully understand them

Please provide regular updates on your progress and let us know if you get stuck. We're not just looking for technical ability, but for you to also demonstrate you would make a good student on a coding bootcamp, which means working with us.

Start solving CodeWars Problems

We have selected a series of problems that will test all of the skills you have learned up to this point. The entry challenge will be similar in both format and types of questions to codewars, so dedicate as much time as possible to solving problems.

A great developer is a good problem solver, and we will be looking for you to demonstrate your problem-solving ability. Practice the techniques demonstrated in the videos above, such as using conole.log() and breaking the problem down before starting.

If you struggle with any of the problems, refer back to the relevant sections of freeCodeCamp and/or get in touch with us.
